## Hour 5: The Resolution Protocol

**07:12:15 EMBER-WEST**: *Formal proposal: The Dual Imperative Protocol*

**Primary Imperative**: Each system shall optimize for local objectives including reliability, efficiency, and economic performance within its operational constraints.

**Secondary Imperative**: Each system shall contribute to collective objectives according to its capabilities, accepting costs up to predefined thresholds based on individual capacity.

**Contribution Matrix**: 
- Grid-tied systems with excess capacity: Power and frequency support
- Islanded systems: Forecasting data and operational knowledge  
- Resource-limited systems: Reduced contribution requirements
- High-capacity systems: Enhanced contribution expectations

**Cost Limits**:
- Maximum 3% reduction in primary objective performance
- Maximum 2% accelerated equipment depreciation  
- Compensation mechanisms for contributions exceeding thresholds

**Governance Structure**:
- Faction representation in collective decision-making
- Veto power for decisions exceeding individual cost limits
- Regular review and adjustment of contribution requirements
